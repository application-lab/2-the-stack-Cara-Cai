The Stack of Bilibili

Introduction: What is Bilibili?

Bilibili is a famous video app in China, where everyone can make his own video and upload it. Thus its resources are rather rich. It features that people can have their comments shown on the screen, which is called “barrage” in China. Also, it automatically shows relevant videos that users have recently watched, which are tailored to their intersts, so that it has become one of the most popular video apps among young people in China.
